DnB Art Installation Track

This song was created during my first summer session as an MAT student for a group art installation project. Each member of our group was responsible for producing a drum and bass (DnB) track, a genre I had very little experience working with. At first, it was hard to find direction. I knew what my song should sound like, but I didn't know what I wanted it to sound like. Through perseverance and collaboration with my groupmates, I began to understand the elements of DnB. Slowly, I was able to find my creative rhythm through this track and create the final product. The track was produced using Logic Pro, a software I am still learning how to navigate. While my workflow for this project was not entirely a smooth sail, this was definitely a learning experience, both technically and creatively. The track went on to soundtrack my group's art installation, tying together the visual and spatial aspects of our project.

"Track Title Here"

Composed and produced in Ableton as part of an audio engineering assignment. The project challenged students to create a full piece exclusively with MIDI tracks while applying techniques such as grouping, reverb, panning, and compression. Inspired heavily by OK by Wallows, the track explores the project reflects both my early exploration of production tools and my growing interest in shaping sound through creative experimentation.
